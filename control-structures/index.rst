***********************
Control-Flow Constructs
***********************

Similar to low-level assembly languages, LLVM's IR consists of sequences
of instructions, that are executed sequentially. The instructions are
grouped together to form *basic blocks*. Each basic block terminates
with an instruction that changes the control flow of the program.
